### Presented Work

**Dongjun Kang (first author), Kyungsu Lee (corresponding author)**  
**Anatomy-Aware Distillation with Memory-Augmented SAM2 for Fracture Detection**

The proposed approach combines a SAM2-based teacher-student structure with memory-augmented distillation to improve fracture detection accuracy and robustness under limited-data conditions.
