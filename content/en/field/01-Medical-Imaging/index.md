Medical AI research at MacsLAB focuses on disease diagnosis, environment improvement, and enhancing the efficiency of medical services. We develop cutting-edge technologies that enable early detection and accurate diagnosis of diseases by creating smartphone-based diagnostic tools, 3D ultrasound imaging platforms, and automated deep learning-based analysis systems. These technologies aim to maximize the potential of AI in the medical field and improve patient treatment outcomes.

<!--more-->

Our medical AI research continuously develops new technologies that enable patient-centered care. By combining advanced imaging technologies with deep learning algorithms, we improve diagnostic accuracy and optimize treatment processes. These technological advancements assist medical professionals in making more precise diagnoses and establishing personalized treatment plans. Furthermore, we improve methods for disease prevention and management through medical data analysis, contributing to the enhancement of patient health. The laboratory's efforts aim to realize the potential of AI in medicine and contribute to a healthier society worldwide.

The research direction of medical AI at MacsLAB is centered on the realization of personalized medical services. For example, research is underway to propose individualized health management and disease prevention programs through AI analysis that combines genetic information with lifestyle data. This approach enables precision medicine that considers individual patient characteristics, contributing to effective disease management and prevention. Simultaneously, the development of automated diagnostic systems using AI serves as a support tool for medical professionals, playing a crucial role in increasing diagnostic accuracy and efficiency. These research efforts expand the application range of AI technology in medicine and provide superior health management solutions.

**Key Technologies Utilized**

- Medical Image Analysis
- Biosignal Processing
- Disease Prediction Models
- Deep Learning-based Diagnosis
- Genetic Algorithms
- Skin Cancer Classification
- Automated Medical Record Analysis
- 3D Imaging and Modeling
