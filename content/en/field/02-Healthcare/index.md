MacsLAB focuses on the development of AI technologies applicable to the medical and healthcare sectors. We develop innovative solutions aimed at providing personalized medical services, including mobile-based diagnostic systems, federated learning for privacy protection, and automated deep learning platforms based on multi-task and few-shot learning for the mobile diagnosis of skin diseases. Our research is centered on providing high-quality diagnostic services while ensuring the protection of patient privacy.
