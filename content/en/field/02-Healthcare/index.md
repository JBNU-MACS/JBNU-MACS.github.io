MacsLAB focuses on the development of AI technologies applicable to the medical and healthcare sectors. We develop innovative solutions aimed at providing personalized medical services, including mobile-based diagnostic systems, federated learning for privacy protection, and automated deep learning platforms based on multi-task and few-shot learning for the mobile diagnosis of skin diseases. Our research is centered on providing high-quality diagnostic services while ensuring the protection of patient privacy.

<!--more-->

At MacsLAB, we are leading innovative changes in the healthcare field using AI. The goal of our research topics is to improve the accessibility of medical services and enhance the treatment experience for patients through AI technology. To this end, we focus on developing personalized diagnostic and treatment solutions while researching secure methods for processing and analyzing patient data. Our research findings play a vital role in increasing the quality of medical services and improving the efficiency of the healthcare system. These efforts at MacsLAB accelerate digital innovation in healthcare and contribute to realizing a better future medical environment.

The research direction of healthcare at MacsLAB involves developing new service models through the convergence of technology and medicine. For instance, we are developing AI systems that analyze health data collected from wearable devices in real-time to provide users with personalized exercise and dietary recommendations. This supports the formation of healthy lifestyle habits for users and can contribute to the prevention of chronic diseases in the long term. Additionally, developing AI-based platforms to improve communication between medical professionals and patients is one of our primary research areas. These studies explore the potential of AI in healthcare and contribute to providing user-centered health management solutions through the integration of technology and medical services.

**Key Technologies Utilized**

- Wearable Data Analysis
- Healthcare Big Data Analysis
- Personalized Healthcare Systems
- Predictive Analytics
- Mobile Health Monitoring
- Medical Natural Language Processing (NLP)
- Health Information Security
- Behavior Recognition and Analysis
