The MACS Lab at Jeonbuk National University is looking for **Master's and PhD (Integrated) students** for the Fall 2026 semester.

We welcome applications from those who wish to conduct research ranging from AI theory to practical applications and development.

<!--more-->

## Research Areas

- Medical AI (Medical Image Analysis, Diagnosis Support, Multi-modal Medical Data)
- Vision & Language based Multi-modal AI
- Generalization AI such as Domain Adaptation, Test-time Adaptation, Federated Learning
- Applied AI in Remote Sensing, Aerospace, and Contents
- AI-based Full-Stack System and Service Development
- AI + Mathematics/Optimization/Statistics-based Modeling

## Qualifications

- Those who want to consistently conduct research in Master's/PhD programs
- Those who want to systematically proceed with paper reading/implementation/experimentation/analysis
- Those who enjoy technically defining and solving real-world problems
- Those who can participate responsibly in collaboration and communication

## Opportunities

- Experience the entire research process from topic discovery to experimental design and paper writing
- Mentoring aimed at submission to international conferences/journals
- Project-based practical development and deployment experience
- Customized research guidance connecting individual interests with lab directions

## How to Apply

- Email: **ksl@jbnu.ac.kr**
- Subject line example: `[Graduate Application] Name / Program (Master's, PhD, Integrated)`
- Please attach/include the following in your email:
  - CV (Free format)
  - Brief self-introduction and research interests
  - (Optional) Portfolio, GitHub, papers written/participated in, project links

If you are interested, please feel free to email us or visit the lab.
