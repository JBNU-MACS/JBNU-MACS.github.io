---
title: (Fall 2024) Awarded at K-Health Medical AI Hackathon
date: 2024-11-20
---

Congratulations!

Undergraduate researchers Dayoung Kang, Sumin Kim, Sehyun Park, and Seo-Yeon Choi from MacsLAB won the Grand Prize at the 2024 K-Health Medical AI Hackathon.

The competition focused on developing an AI model for breast mass segmentation using mammography image data. The students developed, trained, and tuned their own segmentation model, achieving high validation performance by utilizing public datasets in addition to the provided dataset, leading to their victory.
