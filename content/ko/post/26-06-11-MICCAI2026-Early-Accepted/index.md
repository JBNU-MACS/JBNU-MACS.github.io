We are pleased to announce that two papers from MACS Lab have been accepted to **Medical Image Computing and Computer Assisted Intervention 2026 (MICCAI 2026)** as **early accepts**, placing them in the **top 9%** of submissions.

The accepted papers are:

- **Dynamic Sub-domain Modeling for Robust Medical Image Segmentation**
- **Uncertainty-Aware Bayesian Prompt Adaptation for Robust Cross-Modality Medical Segmentation**

Congratulations to all authors on this outstanding achievement. MACS Lab will continue advancing robust and clinically meaningful medical AI research.
