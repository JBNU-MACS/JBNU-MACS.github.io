About the Laboratory

Research and Operating Principles

MACS Lab pursues applied AI research grounded in rigorous evaluation, domain knowledge, and implementation. The English page is structured as a concise institutional overview rather than a promotional landing page.

Overview

Location
Jeonju, Republic of Korea
Institution
Jeonbuk National University
Core Scope
Medical AI, multimodal learning, computational science

Mission

We develop AI methods and systems that are technically rigorous, experimentally validated, and practically relevant to medical, scientific, and industrial problem settings.

Research Agenda

  • Medical AI, image analysis, and clinical decision support
  • Vision-language and multimodal learning
  • Optimization, adaptation, personalization, and representation learning
  • Deployable engineering workflows connected to research outputs

Operating Model

  • Research linked to implementation and reproducible evaluation
  • Paper reading, code review, and collaborative experimentation
  • Publication-oriented work with attention to software quality

Collaboration

The laboratory welcomes collaboration in academic research, industry-linked development, and student research training.
