About The Laboratory

Medical AI & Computational Science

MACS Lab studies domain-oriented AI systems that connect medical imaging, multimodal learning, statistical modeling, and deployable engineering.

Research Domains

  • Medical AI and Medical Imaging
  • Vision-Language and Multimodal Intelligence
  • Computational Science and Applied Engineering

Core Methods

  • Domain Adaptation and Test-Time Adaptation
  • Federated Learning and Representation Learning
  • Optimization, Statistics, and Model Personalization

Operating Direction

  • Balanced execution of theory, implementation, and evaluation
  • Research connected to usable systems and software
  • International publication and collaboration oriented workflow
